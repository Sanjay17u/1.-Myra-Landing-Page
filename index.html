About us

Myra, a Western fashion online retail company built on a legacy of quality and craftsmanship. We infuse traditional ranch styles with a Myra twist to create timeless pieces that are made to last. We offer a wide range of country and western wear products from Bags (Tooled Western Sandals, Western Booties & Cowgirl Boots, Vintage & Upcycled Canvas, Concealed Carry and Shoulder Bag Styles) to Shoes (Tooled Sandals, Booties & Boots) & Accessories like Wallets, Western Turquoise Jewelry, Western Keyfobs & more. We also provide an e-gift card option for you to celebrate every occasion. Our collections are handcrafted crafted by skilled artisans, emphasizing a signature collection in Hand-Tooled & Leather products.
